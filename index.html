The Post-Anniversary Challenge

Anniversary campaigns are powerful—but they have a shelf life. The 50th celebration created visibility, goodwill, and a narrative moment. Now SamTrans needs to convert that awareness into sustained behavior change: more riders, more advocates, and stronger community investment in transit's future.

That requires integrated marketing—not just advertising, but the orchestration of every touchpoint, every partner, and every message working in concert.

The Program Manager's Role

  • Aligning creative, media, and digital teams around one strategic narrative
  • Managing agency relationships, scopes, and deliverables across the campaign calendar
  • Translating executive vision into executable project plans with clear owners
  • Measuring what matters and adapting quickly when channels underperform
  • Being the connective tissue between marketing, operations, and community affairs
